About Me

Growing up, I desperately searched for the answer to a simple yet profound question: "What do you want to do when you grow up?". With a knack and passion for problem-solving, I desperately searched for the answer to this question; I eventually found it in a high school computer science classroom.

Ever since, I have spearheaded side projects, attempted endless competitive programming problems, and applied myself through internships at companies such as OpenText and ITSP to pursue my passion for programming.

In my free time, I enjoy a diverse range of activities, from playing basketball and table tennis to mastering piano songs that I see in movies/shows.
